Job


I work as a Functional Game Tester at Activision Games as of June 2020.
Among my roles are to go into a current or upcoming game and look for broken imagery, bad AI scripting, security or legality issues, FPS issues, and more!
Essentially I break the game, report how I broke it, then try to break the newer version!
